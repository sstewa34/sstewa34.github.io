Welcome to Stewart's Sweets, where the warmth of home and the sweetness of tradition come together in every delightful bite. Our story begins with a passion for baking that spans generations, rooted in the cherished recipes passed down from our beloved grandmother, Joan.

At Stewart's Sweets, we believe that a bakery is more than just a place to buy treats - it's a haven where memories are made and flavors are savored. The recipes that form the heart and soul of our menu are a direct legacy from our Irish heritage, infusing each bite with the flavors and warmth of the kitchens that have nourished generations.

Our founder, Bucky Stewart, was raised on the culinary delights of their grandmother's Irish kitchen, where the art of baking was a cherished tradition. Determined to share the magic of those recipes with a wider audience, Stewart's Sweets was born, a bakery that encapsulates the spirit and authenticity of Irish baking.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. Every item on our menu is a labor of love, crafted with the same dedication and attention to detail that Grandma herself exemplified. From the comforting embrace of traditional Irish soda bread to the decadent allure of rich, buttery scones, each creation at Stewart's Sweets is a loving nod to the flavors that have graced Irish tables for centuries.Step into Stewart's Sweets, where the aroma of freshly baked Irish delights beckons, and experience the fusion of family, tradition, and the unmistakable charm of Irish baking. Join us as we invite you to savor the flavors of Ireland, one exquisite bite at a time.
